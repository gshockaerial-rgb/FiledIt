INSTALL FILED IT!
Add Filed It! to your device for the full native app experience — camera access, offline support, and home screen icon.
🤖
Android — Chrome
Tap the button above, or follow these steps:
1
Open this page in Google Chrome on your Android device
2
Tap the ⋮ menu (three dots) in the top-right corner
3
Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
4
Tap Install — the Filed It! icon appears on your home screen
🍎
iPhone / iPad — Safari
iOS requires Safari for PWA installation:
1
Open this page in Safari (not Chrome) on your iPhone or iPad
2
Tap the Share button ⬆️ at the bottom of the screen
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
4
Tap Add — Filed It! appears on your home screen like a native app
💡 Mac Safari Tip
Safari on Mac doesn't support PWA install. Use Chrome or Edge for the full install, or use the download button below to save the app file.
💻
Mac / Desktop — Chrome
Two ways to install in Chrome:
A
Look for the install icon ⊕ at the right end of the Chrome address bar — click it then click Install
B
Or click Chrome's ⋮ menu (top right) → "Cast, save and share" → "Install page as app…" → Install
C
Once installed, Filed It! opens as its own window from your Mac Applications folder or Dock
or
Save the app file directly — open it in Chrome anytime,
even without an internet connection.
